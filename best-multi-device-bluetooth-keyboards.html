Best Multi-Device Bluetooth Keyboards for Seamless Switching

By Karim • May 18, 2026

Juggling multiple devices is the standard for modern workflows. Whether you are switching between a work laptop, a personal desktop, and an iPad, manual re-pairing is an absolute productivity killer. The modern multi-device keyboard completely eliminates this friction, allowing you to move across different operating systems with a single tap. We tested and reviewed the top-selling multi-device keyboards on the market to find the best options for speed, platform compatibility, and daily comfort.

1. The Premium Professional Choice: Logitech MX Keys S

The Logitech MX Keys S is the undisputed king of full-size office setups. It features spherically-dished keys that perfectly hug your fingertips for a highly accurate, near-silent, laptop-like typing feel. It excels at cross-platform productivity, allowing you to pair with up to 3 devices running Windows, macOS, or Linux, and jump between them instantly using the dedicated Easy-Switch keys.

The real magic happens within the Logi Options+ app. With the Smart Actions feature, you can automate complex, repetitive multi-task chains with a single keystroke. Furthermore, when paired with an MX Master 3S mouse, you can use Logitech Flow to drag text, files, and images seamlessly across the screens of entirely separate computers.

2. The Ultra-Portable Budget Pick: Logitech Pebble Keys 2 K380s

If you need a keyboard that can slip into a backpack and navigate a coffee shop or a university lecture hall, the Logitech Pebble Keys 2 K380s is an incredible value proposition. Built heavily out of recycled plastic, this ultra-slim, lightweight keyboard features rounded, scooped keys that offer a quiet and comfortable typing experience on the go.

Despite its budget price tag, it doesn't skimp on multi-device muscle. It uses Bluetooth Low Energy to connect to 3 distinct devices simultaneously—including iPadOS, Android, Windows, and Mac—swapping between them smoothly via dedicated colored Easy-Switch keys. Best of all, it boasts an astonishing 3-year battery life, freeing you completely from charging cables.

3. The Mechanical Enthusiast Pick: NuPhy Air75 V2

For users who refuse to compromise on the crisp, tactile feel of a mechanical keyboard but still need professional multi-device flexibility, the NuPhy Air75 V2 is a masterpiece. Housed in a premium aluminum frame with a striking translucent bottom shell, this low-profile 75% layout board features durable, fade-resistant PBT keycaps and hot-swappable Gateron mechanical switches.

The Air75 V2 pushes connectivity boundaries by supporting up to 4 concurrent devices across Mac, Windows, Linux, Android, and iOS. It offers triple-mode options including ultra-fast 2.4GHz wireless for lag-free gaming and Bluetooth 5.1. It also includes fully open-source QMK/VIA firmware, giving you endless possibilities to remap keys and create custom layered macros via a browser-based console.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does a keyboard switch between devices instantly?

Multi-device keyboards keep separate Bluetooth profiles open in their memory. When you press an Easy-Switch button, the keyboard pauses transmission to the current profile and pairs immediately with the pre-selected device profile, usually taking less than two seconds.

Can I use these keyboards with both a Mac and a Windows PC?

Yes. All three keyboards featured in this guide have printed layouts that cater to both macOS (Command/Option) and Windows (Alt/Win) keys, modifying their inputs automatically based on the device active at the time.
