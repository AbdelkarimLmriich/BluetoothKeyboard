Best iPad Pro Magic Keyboard Alternatives

By The Hardware Team • May 2024

Apple's official Magic Keyboard is beautiful, but dropping $300+ on an accessory is tough to justify. Fortunately, third-party manufacturers have successfully reverse-engineered the magnetic floating cantilever design. Here are the absolute best iPad Pro keyboard cases that give you the premium Macbook experience for a fraction of the cost.

Top Picks at a Glance

  • Best Premium Clone: ESR Rebound Magnetic Case
  • Best Ultra-Portable: Logitech Pebble Keys 2

1. The Best Magic Keyboard Clone: ESR Rebound Magnetic Case

If you want the exact look, feel, and floating design of the official Apple Magic Keyboard, the ESR Rebound is the undisputed king. It features a genuine floating cantilever magnetic stand, a multi-touch trackpad that perfectly supports iPadOS gestures, and backlit keys.

2. The Ultra-Portable Option: Logitech Pebble Keys 2

Sometimes you don't want a heavy case permanently attached to your iPad. If you prefer to keep your tablet light and only bring out a keyboard when it is time to type, the Logitech Pebble Keys 2 is unbeatable. It slips easily into any bag and switches seamlessly between your iPad and phone.
