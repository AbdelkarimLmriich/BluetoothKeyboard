Frequently Asked Questions

Do 60% wireless keyboards have arrow keys?

Physically, dedicated independent arrow keys are removed from a 60% chassis to save desk room. However, they remain instantly accessible via a secondary hardware layer by holding down the designated FN utility key combo.

Is wireless transmission safe for competitive esports tournaments?

Yes. Modern pro-grade 2.4GHz protocols like Logitech Lightspeed and SteelSeries Quantum 2.0 operate on high-speed 1ms polling intervals, matching or exceeding the speed parameters of standard wired connections.
