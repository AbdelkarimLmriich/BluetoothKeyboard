Best Standalone Wireless Numpads for TKL and 60% Keyboard Setups

By Karim • May 18, 2026

For enthusiasts and professionals who use compact 60%, 65%, or TKL keyboards for gaming or minimalist aesthetics, losing the number pad is a necessary sacrifice for desk space. However, when it comes time for heavy data entry, accounting, or setting up custom macro layouts, that missing numpad becomes a major bottleneck. The solution isn't to replace your primary keyboard, but to augment it with a dedicated, standalone wireless number pad. These wireless modules give you the flexibility to place them on either side of your desk when needed, and easily stash them away when you're done. We reviewed the absolute best wireless numpads on the market designed to supercharge your compact setup.

With hot-swappable Gateron Jupiter Brown switches and premium acoustic foam layering, it delivers an unparalleled, premium typing feel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a wireless numpad on the left side of my keyboard?

Yes! One of the biggest advantages of a standalone wireless numpad is flexibility. Placing it on the left side allows you to type numbers or trigger macros with your left hand while keeping your right hand completely free on your mouse.

Do these wireless numpads support fully customizable macros?

Models that feature open-source firmware like QMK/VIA (such as Keychron) allow you to remap every single key on any layer, converting the pad into a fully functional macro deck for editing or gaming.
