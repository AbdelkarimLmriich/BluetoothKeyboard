Frequently Asked Questions

Can a $20 Bluetooth keyboard actually last a whole semester?

Absolutely. Unlike complex mechanical keyboards, these budget membrane and scissor-switch boards have very few moving parts. As long as you aren't dropping them down a flight of stairs, they will easily survive four years of college.

Do I need a USB dongle to connect these to an iPad or MacBook?

No. All the keyboards on this list use pure Bluetooth connectivity. There are no USB dongles to lose in your backpack, which is perfect for modern MacBooks and iPads that lack traditional USB-A ports.

Are these budget keyboards quiet enough for a university library?

Yes. Budget boards utilizing membrane domes or scissor switches (like the iClever and Logitech options) operate at very low decibel levels (under 50dB). They are significantly quieter than standard mechanical keyboards and will not earn you dirty looks in the quiet study zones.
