Why Choose a Mechanical Keyboard?

Unlike standard membrane keyboards, mechanical keyboards use individual switches for every key. This provides tactile feedback, faster actuation, and a significantly longer lifespan.

"Once you switch to a high-quality mechanical keyboard, typing on a standard laptop feels like drumming your fingers on a piece of cardboard." - Tech Reviewer

Switch Types Explained

  • Linear (Red): Smooth and quiet, ideal for gaming.
  • Tactile (Brown): A slight bump on actuation, great for typing and general use.
  • Clicky (Blue): Loud and clicky, satisfying but potentially annoying in office environments.
