Frequently Asked Questions

Will these keyboards work directly with my specific Smart TV?

Most modern Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony) support standard USB and Bluetooth HID keyboard inputs. However, certain native apps (like YouTube or Netflix on older TV operating systems) restrict keyboard inputs within their specific search fields. In those cases, the trackpad cursor will still navigate the primary system layout smoothly.

What is the difference between the 2.4GHz USB and Bluetooth models for a TV?

2.4GHz models use an included USB plug that works instantly with absolute zero setup. Bluetooth versions require a quick menu pairing process but do not require an open USB port, which is perfect for slim wall-mounted displays or devices like iPads and streaming pucks.
