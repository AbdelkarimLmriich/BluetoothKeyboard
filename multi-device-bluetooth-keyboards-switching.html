Best Multi-Device Bluetooth Keyboards with Device Switching

By Karim • May 18, 2026

If your desk is cluttered with a work laptop, a personal PC, and a smartphone, using a different keyboard for each is a massive drain on your productivity. The solution is a multi-device Bluetooth keyboard. These intelligent peripherals allow you to pair with up to three different devices simultaneously across multiple operating systems (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android). With the simple tap of a dedicated 'Easy-Switch' button, you can instantly seamlessly transition from typing an email on your monitor to replying to a text message on your phone. We tested the best multi-device keyboards that will instantly declutter your workspace.

1. The Best for Heavy Desktop Use: Logitech K780

The Logitech K780 is designed to be the absolute command center of a heavy-duty workstation. Unlike flimsy portable options, the K780 is intentionally weighty and robust. The standout feature is the integrated heavy rubber cradle running along the top edge. It is strong enough to securely hold a heavy iPad Pro and a smartphone completely upright at the perfect viewing angle while you type.

It features a full-size layout complete with a number pad for heavy data entry, utilizing Logitech's incredibly quiet, circular membrane keys. You can connect via Bluetooth Smart or the included USB Unifying receiver. Switching between your PC, Mac, and the tablet sitting in the cradle is instantaneous using the three dedicated white Easy-Switch keys.

2. The Best for Portable Aesthetics: Logitech Pebble Keys 2 K380s

If you want multi-device functionality but need something that looks incredibly clean and minimalist, the Logitech Pebble Keys 2 K380s is the undisputed champion. This keyboard has taken over aesthetic workspace setups on TikTok and Instagram for good reason. It is incredibly slim, portable, and made with up to 49% recycled plastic.

Despite its small size, it packs massive functionality. The three Easy-Switch buttons let you instantly hop across your laptop, tablet, and phone. Using the Logi Options+ app, you can customize the top function row to instantly open the emoji menu, capture your screen, or execute shortcuts. Best of all, it runs on two AAA batteries that provide an astonishing 3-year battery life, meaning you'll almost never have to think about charging it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does multi-device switching cause input lag?

No. Once the devices are initially paired, pressing the Easy-Switch button takes about one to two seconds to reconnect to the new device. After that connection is made, typing is completely lag-free.

Can I copy and paste between two different computers?

Yes, but it requires Logitech Flow. If both computers are on the same Wi-Fi network and running the Logi Options+ app, Logitech Flow allows you to drag your mouse and copy text from a Mac directly onto a Windows PC using these keyboards.
