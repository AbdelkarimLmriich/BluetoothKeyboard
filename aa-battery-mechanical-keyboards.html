Best Mechanical Keyboards with AA Batteries (Longest Life)

By The Hardware Team•May 2024

Internal lithium-ion batteries degrade over time, turning your expensive mechanical keyboard into a wired paperweight after a few years. For true longevity, standard AAA/AA battery-powered boards are king. Here are the best hot-swappable and pre-built options that run for months on standard batteries.

Top Picks at a Glance

  • Best Budget Kit: CIY Tester68
  • Best Pre-built & Colorful: Logitech POP Keys

1. The Best Barebones Kit: CIY Tester68

The CIY Tester68 is a legend in the budget mechanical keyboard space. It is a barebones kit (you add your own switches and keycaps) that runs off two AAA batteries. It provides a lag-free 2.4GHz dongle and Bluetooth 5.0, making it the perfect customizable daily driver.

2. The Colorful Option: Logitech POP Keys

If you want a mechanical keyboard that lasts forever but brings personality to your desk, the Logitech POP Keys is fantastic. It features typewriter-style mechanical keys, connects instantly via Bluetooth, and runs on two AAA batteries that Logitech claims will last up to 36 months.
