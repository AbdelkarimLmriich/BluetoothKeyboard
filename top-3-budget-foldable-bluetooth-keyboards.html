Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to buy Bluetooth keyboards directly from AliExpress?

Yes, provided you buy from highly-rated, certified factory storefronts. The exact same hardware is often rebranded by Western sellers on Amazon at a 30% to 50% markup. Sourcing direct simply means trading fast 2-day shipping for massive price arbitrage.

Do these budget foldable keyboards work with Apple devices (iPhone/iPad)?

Absolutely. All three models reviewed here utilize universal Bluetooth chips that pair flawlessly with iOS, iPadOS, macOS, Android, and Windows. Note that to use a trackpad model (like the Jomaa) on an iPad, you must enable "AssistiveTouch" in your accessibility settings.

How durable are the hinges on cheap folding keyboards?

Modern bi-fold and tri-fold keyboards use reinforced magnetic hinges. While they are highly resilient against being crushed in a backpack, they are designed to be typed on across a hard, flat surface (like a desk or airplane tray). Typing with them balanced unevenly on your lap can stress the central bridge over time.
