Best Compact Bluetooth Keyboards for Steam Deck Docked Setups

By The Hardware Team • May 2024

A massive, bulky keyboard completely ruins the minimalist appeal of a docked Steam Deck setup. If you are hooking your handheld up to a monitor for desktop mode or strategy games, you need a compact, reliable Bluetooth board. After testing numerous layouts, here are the absolute best options for SteamOS.

Top Picks at a Glance

  • Best Overall: Keychron K3 Pro
  • Best with Trackpad: Logitech K400 Plus

1. The Overall Best: Keychron K3 Pro (Low Profile)

The Keychron K3 Pro is the holy grail for Steam Deck users. It is an ultra-slim, 75% layout mechanical keyboard that fits perfectly in a small tech pouch alongside your Deck. It features QMK/VIA support so you can remap keys specifically for Linux/SteamOS gaming, and connects flawlessly via Bluetooth 5.1.

2. The Couch Gaming Option: Logitech K400 Plus

If you are docking your Steam Deck to a living room TV, a standard keyboard won't cut it. You need a built-in mouse. The Logitech K400 Plus features an integrated trackpad, allowing you to navigate SteamOS desktop mode from the comfort of your couch without needing a separate mouse and mousepad.
