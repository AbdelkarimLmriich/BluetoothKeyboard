Frequently Asked Questions

Are retro typewriter keyboards comfortable for long typing sessions?

Yes, because they use authentic mechanical key switches that provide excellent tactile feedback. However, because the circular keycaps have more physical space between them than standard square keys, touch-typists may experience a small learning curve for the first few days.

Do these keyboards click loudly?

Models like the Qwerkywriter 2 and Adventurers use clicky blue-style switches to mimic the acoustic sound of actual old typewriters. If you want a quieter alternative that won't distract others, the Logitech POP Keys uses a softer, more muffled tactile switch profile.
