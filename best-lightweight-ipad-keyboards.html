Best Lightweight iPad Keyboards for Travel (That Don't Cost $300)

By Karim • May 18, 2026

Apple’s official Magic Keyboard is a beautiful piece of hardware, but asking mobile writers, students, and travelers to drop nearly $300 on a heavy case that doubles the weight of their tablet is a tough pill to swallow. Whether you are typing out emails on a tray table at 30,000 feet or drafting a chapter in a crowded coffee shop, we reviewed the absolute best lightweight travel keyboards for iPad users.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will these third-party keyboards support native iPad keyboard shortcuts?

Yes. When paired over Bluetooth, these keyboards fully trigger standard iPadOS commands like Command + Tab to switch apps or Command + Space to open Spotlight search. Decks like the Keys-To-Go 2 and MX Mini feature explicit printed Mac/Apple layout legends out of the box.

How can I protect my keyboard keys from breaking inside a travel bag?

Loose items inside a tight bag can easily snap off exposed keycaps. Choosing a keyboard with an integrated cover plate (like the Keys-To-Go 2) or sliding your slim deck inside a dedicated neoprene tablet sleeve is the best way to safeguard your hardware switches on the move.
